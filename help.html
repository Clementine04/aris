HELP: HOW TO PLAY

Welcome, Warrior! Your mission is to destroy the incoming words before they reach the bottom of the screen. Speed and accuracy are your only weapons.

Game Mechanics

  • Identify the Target: When words start dropping from the top, they are considered active targets.
  • Start Typing: You do not need to click on a word. Simply start typing the word into the input box exactly as it appears.
  • Focus Lock: Once you type the first correct letter of a word, your focus locks onto that word. The letters you've typed will be visually marked (e.g., highlighted or changed color) on the word itself.
  • Destroy the Enemy: After successfully typing the entire word, hit the SPACEBAR or ENTER key to destroy it instantly. This earns you points and makes you ready to target the next word.
  • Scoring: Points are awarded based on the speed and length of the word you destroy. Longer words offer higher rewards.

Game Over Conditions

  • Timer: Each player will be given 60 seconds to attack the fallen words. Each popped words will turn into points.
  • Excessive Errors: Making too many incorrect keystrokes will also end your run. Accuracy matters!

Strategy Tips

Prioritize words that are closer to the bottom to maximize your run time. Always keep your fingers ready on the home row!
